ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Shi Tong (Steph)!

I am currently pursuing a MSc in Technology Management and have attained Bachelor of Engineering Science (Computer Engineering). I am part of a Premier Scholarship Programme, Renaissance Engineering Programme (REP), in Nanyang Technological University (NTU), which aims to groom multi-disciplinary Engineering students in a Business environment. Hence, I am equipped with the hard and soft skills to put on different hats as both an Engineer and Business graduate. My internship (startups) and project experiences have varied from Software Engineering, Consulting and UI/UX Designing.

As part of REP, I spent a concurrent year abroad (2018-2019) taking Business and Computer Science modules at UC Berkeley. I also spent the summer interning as a Software Engineer at a digital incubator in Silicon Valley.

I am passionate and believe in building/designing products to empower and provide value to people's lives. With my background and experiences in the 3 fields - Technology, Business Consulting and UI/UX Design, I seek to realise my belief.

Hobbies: Dancing, Learning new languages (French, Korean, Spanish) Reading, Travelling, Volleyball
